WHAT IS GENSHINPLACE?

GenshinPlace is a community built because of a collaborative project and social experiment
hosted on the social networking site known as Reddit.
This project was known as r/place.
r/place brought together over four thousand people and nestled them into a single community that is now known as GenshinPlace.
The members of Genshin place all worked together to put Genshin Impact onto the r/place canvas.
They made allies and enemies and worked hard to create the masterpiece they did.
